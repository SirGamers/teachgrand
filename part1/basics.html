What is a keyboard?

A keyboard is one of the most fundamental tool of your computer. It allows you to type in letters and words into a search engine (later) and search different things.
Next is the mouse, this allows you to have a pointer like tool that you can click on things with to take you to new webpages(later concept).

What is a mouse?

The mouse is very simple to use. You move the mouse across the mousepad to move the "cursor" across the screen.
Press the left side to "click" on things. Click and hold on texts or pictures to "highlight" them, which allows for a plethora of actions.
